    Approved Abbreviations and Acronyms¶

    Purpose¶

    This appendix records recurring abbreviations and acronyms used throughout the repository. The aim is to reduce uncontrolled variation and avoid unnecessary expansion where the abbreviated form is already standard in engineering documentation.

    General rule¶

    At first significant occurrence in a page, provide the full form unless the acronym is universally recognised within the intended audience. After that, maintain the chosen form consistently.

    Core examples¶

    Form Expansion Usage note
    C2 Command and Control Use in defence-oriented or programme context when relevant
    CAPA Corrective and Preventive Action Use only where the reader is expected to know quality terminology
    EMI Electromagnetic Interference Prefer the abbreviated form after first definition
    FAT Factory Acceptance Test Define once, then reuse as FAT
    KPI Key Performance Indicator Acceptable in reporting and programme language
    MTTR Mean Time To Repair Useful in maintenance and supportability contexts
    NCR Non-Conformance Report Use when the documentary context clearly involves quality review
    SOP Standard Operating Procedure Prefer only when the repository page discusses procedures directly
    TBD To Be Defined Use carefully; do not allow it to replace real engineering decisions

    Editorial caution¶

    Do not create local acronyms unnecessarily. A page becomes harder to review when the writer abbreviates terms that appear only once or that are not standard for the reader group.
