# Review Playbooks

!!! note "Playbook use note"
    A playbook is most useful when applied in sequence. Follow the review order rather than correcting isolated sentences without a framework.

!!! abstract "Section purpose"
    The playbooks in this section convert general quality expectations into repeatable review behaviour. They are especially useful during the move from draft-level classroom output to repository-level technical documentation.

## Why playbooks matter

Many learners attempt to improve a document by making isolated sentence changes. This often produces surface-level improvement without increasing technical value. A playbook provides an ordered method: what to check first, what to standardise next, and how to decide whether the page is ready for reuse.

## Recommended review order

1. confirm scope and audience;
2. test terminology consistency;
3. verify sentence-level precision;
4. inspect documentary structure;
5. check internal links and repository fit;
6. perform final style and formatting review.
